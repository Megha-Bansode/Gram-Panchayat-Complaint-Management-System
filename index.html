Jal Jeevan Mission

Delivering clean and sufficient drinking water through functional household tap connections to every rural household by 2024.

Learn More

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct financial benefit of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments directly into the bank accounts of eligible small farmers.

Learn More

Free health cover up to ₹5 Lakhs per family for secondary & tertiary hospitalization.

Eligibility: SECC-listed BPL families. Learn More

About Us

About Gram Panchayat

The Gram Panchayat is committed to transparent governance, public participation, digital services, and rural development. Citizens can register complaints, track progress, access schemes, and stay informed through this portal.

  • Transparent and accountable local governance for every citizen
  • Digital complaint registration with real-time status tracking
  • Access to all Central & State government welfare schemes
  • Online certificates, tax payments and public notices
  • 24×7 citizen support with emergency helpline numbers

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about registering, tracking, and resolving complaints.

Click "Register Complaint" on the hero section or the Services card. Log in with Citizen credentials, choose the department, fill in complaint details, attach photos if needed, and submit. You'll receive a unique Complaint ID instantly via SMS.

Resolution time varies by category. Critical issues like streetlights, sewage, or water supply are usually resolved within 3–5 working days. Complex structural issues may take up to 15 working days. The system updates status at each step.

Enter your Complaint ID (e.g., GPCMS202600123) in the "Complaint Status Checker" section on this page and click Track. A live timeline will show all stages from submission to resolution.

We list 36+ schemes including PM Awas Yojana, Jal Jeevan Mission, PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, Ayushman Bharat, MGNREGA, Ujjwala Yojana, Swachh Bharat Mission, and many more. Visit the Government Schemes section to explore all.

Yes, Aadhaar-based OTP verification is required for first-time registration to prevent spam and ensure authenticity. However, tracking an existing Complaint ID is open to all without login.
